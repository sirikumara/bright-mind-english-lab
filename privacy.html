Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 17, 2026

Bright Mind English Lab We operate the Bright Mind English Lab, which facilitates learning through social media like a Pinterest board: BrightMindPlus. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when users interact with our services.

Information We Collect

We may collect limited information when users interact with our website or services, including:

  • Basic technical information such as browser type, device type, and IP address.
  • Usage information such as pages visited, time spent on the site, and interactions with our content.
  • Information related to API interactions when authorized by users.

We do not intentionally collect sensitive personal information.

How We Use the Information

The collected information may be used for the following purposes:

  • To provide and maintain our services.
  • To improve the quality and functionality of our website and educational content.
  • To analyze usage patterns and performance.
  • To publish or manage content using third-party APIs such as the Pinterest API.
  • To ensure security and prevent misuse of our services.

Data Retention

We retain collected data only for as long as necessary to operate and improve our services. Basic analytics data may be stored for a limited period to help us understand how users interact with our content.

Third-Party Services

Our services may integrate with third-party platforms and APIs, mostly related to social media. These services may process data according to their own privacy policies. We do not sell or rent personal information to third parties.

Data Sharing

We do not share personal information with third parties except in the following cases:

  • When required by law or legal process.
  • When necessary to operate integrated services such as APIs.
  • To protect the security and integrity of our services.

Security

We take reasonable measures to protect information from unauthorized access, misuse, or disclosure.

Children's Privacy

Our services are intended for general audiences and educational purposes. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
